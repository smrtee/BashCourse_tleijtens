Adopt An Ape

Caring for one of our apes costs over $30,000 a year. By symbolically adopting the ape of your choice for a one-time donation of $300 or $400, you are helping ensure your orangutan or chimpanzee's health, safety, and well-being. Your donation will go toward the basic items your ape needs, such as food, medical care, habitat maintenance, and enrichment activities vital to these intelligent and sensitive individuals.

FAQs

Thank you for being a part of our adoptive parent family!

All virtual visits are subject to the sanctuary's and the apes' schedules. Not every ape's personality will allow for a live virtual visit. In some instances, a prerecorded session may be needed. Virtual Visits are scheduled by appointment only.

All private tours are scheduled on Thursday, Friday, or Saturday, depending on our availability. Tours last approximately 2 hours.

**Virtual visits and private tours are valid for one year from the initial donation date.

Have questions about adopting an ape? Need help choosing which ape you would like to adopt or scheduling your virtual visit/private tour? Contact Donor Programs Coordinator, Lauren at donorprograms@centerforgreatapes.org or call the Donor Relations Office at 863-767-8903 ext. 230.
